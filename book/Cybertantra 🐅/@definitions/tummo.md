# Tummo

A Tibetan concept referring both to the goddess of heat and passion, and to a specific form of tantric meditation practice focused on generating and controlling inner heat.

## Practice
- Form of tantric meditation
- Focused on accessing Inner Fire
- Similar to Indian tantric practices
- Rooted in Aryan spiritual tradition

## Significance
- Manifestation of Shakti energy
- Tool for spiritual transformation
- Method for awakening inner power
- Bridge between physical and spiritual realms
