# Shakti

The active, creative force that drives birth and destruction at every level of the universe. In Hindu philosophy, Shakti represents the divine feminine energy and the dynamic power that underlies all of existence. She is the counterpart to Shiva and represents the active principle of reality, while Shiva represents the passive, contemplative aspect.

## Context
- Primary force in the Master Age of Shakti
- Manifests through various forms, most notably as Kali
- Essential for understanding the cycles of creation and destruction
