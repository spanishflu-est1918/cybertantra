# Consciousness

The fundamental awareness that underlies all experience and existence. In tantric philosophy, consciousness is not merely awareness of thoughts but encompasses all levels of being from pure spirit to material reality.

## Levels
- Pure unified awareness (Satya Yuga state)
- Mental/intellectual awareness (Treta Yuga state)
- Emotional/passionate awareness (Dvapara Yuga state)
- Material/physical awareness (Kali Yuga state)

## Characteristics
- Can exist in different states of unity or differentiation
- Moves through cycles (Yugas)
- Can be cultivated and transformed
- Foundation of spiritual practice
