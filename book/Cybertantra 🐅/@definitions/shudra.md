# Shudra

In traditional caste system, those naturally oriented toward direct physical experience and practical work. In modern context, often refers to consciousness focused solely on material reality.

## Traditional Role
- Practical workers
- Direct experience
- Physical reality
- Service orientation

## Modern Context
- Material consciousness
- Programmed thinking
- Passive consumption
- Resistance to challenge

## Transformation
- Potential for awakening
- Need for authentic experience
- Path to higher consciousness
- Integration of wisdom
