# NPC (Non-Player Character)

In spiritual context, refers to individuals operating purely on programmed responses without authentic consciousness or individual will. Borrowed from gaming terminology.

## Characteristics
- Programmed responses
- Lack of individual thought
- Resistance to new ideas
- Preference for authority

## Spiritual Context
- Unawakened consciousness
- Mechanical behavior
- Automated responses
- Lack of authentic being

## Transformation
- Potential for awakening
- Need for authentic experience
- Possibility of consciousness
- Path to individuation
