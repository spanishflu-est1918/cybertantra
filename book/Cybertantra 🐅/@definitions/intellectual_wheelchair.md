# Intellectual Wheelchair

Metaphor for the state of intellectual dependency and inability to stand on one's own mental feet. Represents the comfort of received wisdom over challenging personal thought.

## Characteristics
- Dependency on others' thinking
- Fear of intellectual challenge
- Comfort in passive learning
- Resistance to difficult ideas

## Manifestations
- Reliance on authority
- Fear of original thought
- Addiction to easy answers
- Avoidance of mental effort

## Liberation
- Facing difficult ideas
- Developing original thought
- Embracing challenge
- Building intellectual strength

## Cultural Context
- Academic conformity
- Intellectual laziness
- Fear of controversy
- Passive consumption of ideas
