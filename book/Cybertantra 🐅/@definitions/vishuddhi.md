# Vishuddhi

The throat chakra and principle of mind/intellect in tantric philosophy. Associated with communication, expression, and the air element.

## Characteristics
- Center of mental activity and intellectual expression
- Regulates and balances other energies
- Connected to communication and truth
- Can be corrupted into mind control and propaganda

## Modern Context
- Often manipulated through mass media
- Key battleground for consciousness
- Must be balanced with other centers
- Critical for spiritual development
