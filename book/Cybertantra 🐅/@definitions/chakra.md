# Chakra

Energy centers within the subtle body that process and distribute different forms of spiritual and vital energy. The term literally means "wheel" or "disc" in Sanskrit.

## Major Chakras
1. Muladhara (root) - survival, stability
2. Svadhisthana (sacral) - creativity, sexuality
3. Manipura (solar plexus) - power, transformation
4. Anahata (heart) - love, integration
5. Vishuddhi (throat) - expression, communication
6. Ajna (third eye) - insight, vision
7. Sahasrara (crown) - divine connection

## Function
- Energy processing centers
- Consciousness transformers
- Points of power
- Integration nodes

## Practice
- Activated through yoga
- Balanced through meditation
- Developed through pranayama
- Integrated through tantra
