# Kshatriya

The warrior-leader caste in traditional society, representing those naturally oriented toward protection, leadership, and maintaining order through strength.

## Characteristics
- Warrior spirit
- Leadership ability
- Protective nature
- Strategic mind

## Traditional Role
- Military leadership
- Social protection
- Maintaining order
- Executive power

## Modern Context
- Need for authentic leadership
- Balance of force and wisdom
- Protection of values
- Strategic action

## Virtues
- Courage
- Honor
- Discipline
- Strategic wisdom
- Protection of dharma
