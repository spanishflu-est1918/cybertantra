# Mother Principle

The fundamental creative and nurturing force in the universe, represented by the divine feminine in various forms (Shakti, Kali, etc.). Associated with direct experience and natural power.

## Aspects
- Creative force
- Nurturing energy
- Natural power
- Direct experience

## Manifestations
- Shakti (divine power)
- Kali (transformation)
- Primal nature
- Life force

## Significance
- Opposition to patriarchal control
- Source of authentic power
- Connection to nature
- Basis for spiritual liberation
