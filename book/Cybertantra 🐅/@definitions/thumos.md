# Thumos

The ancient Greek concept of spirited energy, similar to the Inner Fire in Eastern traditions. Represents the passionate force of the warrior spirit and noble anger.

## Characteristics
- Warrior spirit
- Noble passion
- Righteous anger
- Vital energy

## Historical Context
- Central to Greek philosophy
- Similar to Eastern Inner Fire
- Part of tripartite soul (Plato)
- Warrior virtue

## Modern Application
- Source of motivation
- Driving force for change
- Basis for authentic action
- Connection to ancestral power
