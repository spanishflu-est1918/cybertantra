# Shiva

The principle of pure consciousness and destruction in Hindu philosophy. Represents the passive, witnessing aspect of reality in contrast to Shakti's active force.

## Aspects
- Pure consciousness
- Destroyer of ignorance
- Yogic master
- Divine masculine

## Relationship to Shakti
- Complementary opposite
- Static/passive principle
- Consciousness to Shakti's power
- Unity in duality

## Significance
- Master of yoga
- Transcendent awareness
- Ultimate reality
- Pattern of spiritual mastery
