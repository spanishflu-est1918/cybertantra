# Vedas

The oldest sacred texts of the Hindu tradition, comprising a vast body of ancient spiritual knowledge. The term literally means "knowledge" in Sanskrit.

## Significance
- Foundation texts of Hindu philosophy
- Contains hymns, philosophical discussions, and ritual knowledge
- Preserved through oral tradition before being written
- Predated by some traditions (like Kali worship)

## Content Categories
- Mantras (sacred sounds)
- Brahmanas (ritual instructions)
- Aranyakas (forest texts)
- Upanishads (philosophical treatises)

## Modern Context
- Reference point for traditional knowledge
- Source of spiritual practices
- Foundation for understanding Hindu cosmology
- Often subject to interpretation and distortion
