# Tantra

A spiritual tradition emphasizing direct experience and the transformation of energy. Uses ritual, meditation, and yogic practices to achieve liberation and power.

## Core Principles
- Unity of spirit and matter
- Sacred sexuality
- Energy transformation
- Direct experience

## Practices
- Ritual work
- Energy cultivation
- Meditation
- Deity worship
- Sacred sexuality

## Paths
- Left-Hand Path (vamachara)
- Right-Hand Path (dakshinachara)
- Middle Path (madhyamarga)

## Modern Context
- Often misunderstood
- Powerful transformation tool
- Integration of opposites
- Path to liberation in material world
