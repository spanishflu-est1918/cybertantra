# Yahweh

In the context of the manuscript, represents the principle of patriarchal control and separation from direct spiritual experience. Symbolizes systematic opposition to natural power.

## Symbolism
- Tyrannical patriarch
- Abstract authority
- Separation from nature
- Control through doctrine

## Historical Context
- Opposition to mother principle
- System of control
- Rejection of direct experience
- Institutionalized power

## Modern Manifestation
- Bureaucratic control
- Separation from nature
- Abstract authority
- Suppression of direct experience
