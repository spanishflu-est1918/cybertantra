# Spiritual Materialism

The tendency to use spiritual practices and concepts for ego enhancement rather than genuine transformation. A common corruption of authentic spiritual work.

## Manifestations
- Collection of spiritual experiences
- Spiritual status seeking
- Knowledge without practice
- External show without inner work

## Dangers
- Blocks real transformation
- Creates spiritual ego
- Prevents authentic practice
- Corrupts teaching

## Recognition
- Emphasis on appearance
- Lack of real practice
- Knowledge without experience
- Status-seeking behavior

## Antidotes
- Authentic practice
- Honest self-examination
- Direct experience
- Genuine transformation
