# Yoga

A comprehensive system of spiritual and physical practices aimed at union of individual consciousness with universal consciousness. Far more than just physical exercise.

## Core Aspects
- Physical practices (asana)
- Breathing techniques (pranayama)
- Meditation (dhyana)
- Energy work (kundalini)

## Branches
- Raja Yoga (royal path/meditation)
- Hatha Yoga (forceful/physical)
- Karma Yoga (action/service)
- Bhakti Yoga (devotion)
- Jnana Yoga (knowledge)
- Tantra Yoga (energy/ritual)

## Modern Application
- Tool for transformation
- Method of power cultivation
- Path to self-realization
- System of total development
