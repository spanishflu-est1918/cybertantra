# Aryan/Aryanism

A spiritual and cultural tradition emphasizing nobility of spirit and direct experience of divine power. The term "Arya" originally meant "noble" or "excellent" in Sanskrit.

## Core Principles
- Direct spiritual experience
- Warrior-sage tradition
- Natural hierarchy based on merit
- Balance of power and wisdom

## Historical Context
- Ancient Indo-European spiritual tradition
- Source of various spiritual practices (including Tummo)
- Emphasis on direct experience over dogma
- Opposition to tyrannical religious systems

## Modern Significance
- Focus on authentic spiritual development
- Resistance to materialistic degradation
- Emphasis on natural order
- Balance of spiritual and martial virtues
