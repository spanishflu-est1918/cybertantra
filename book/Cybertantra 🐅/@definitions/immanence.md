# Immanence

The presence of divine or spiritual reality within the material world. Contrasts with transcendence by finding the sacred within rather than beyond physical reality.

## Characteristics
- Divine presence in material world
- Sacred within ordinary reality
- Complementary to transcendence
- Direct experience of spirit in matter

## Significance
- Important in tantric practice
- Basis for sacred materialism
- Key to understanding Shakti
- Essential for balanced spiritual development

## Modern Context
- Often overlooked in favor of pure transcendence
- Important for grounded spirituality
- Connects spirit and matter
- Vital for practical spiritual work
