# Dharma

One's essential nature, inner calling, or true purpose. In the context of spiritual and social development, dharma represents the path one must follow to fulfill their authentic role in existence.

## Key Concepts
- Natural inclination and capacity
- Individual's true nature and purpose
- Not determined by birth but by inner qualities
- Guides proper action and life direction

## Application
- Basis for authentic social organization
- Framework for personal development
- Guide for spiritual practice
- Foundation for meaningful action
