# Bhagavata Purana

An ancient Hindu text focused on devotion to Krishna and containing important prophecies about the Kali Yuga. One of the most significant Puranic texts.

## Historical Context
- Over 3000 years old
- Part of Vedic literature
- Focus on bhakti tradition
- Source of prophecies

## Kali Yuga Predictions
- Decline in spiritual values
- Material obsession
- Social degradation
- Environmental crisis
- Political corruption
- Mental health deterioration
- Family breakdown
- Climate issues

## Significance
- Warning about current age
- Guide for spiritual practice
- Source of devotional teachings
- Historical perspective on present
