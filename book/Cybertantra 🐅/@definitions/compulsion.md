# Compulsion

An uncontrolled urge or drive, often representing unmastered Shakti energy. In spiritual practice, the state of being driven by forces rather than consciously directing them.

## Characteristics
- Unconscious driving force
- Unmastered energy
- Automatic behavior
- Lack of conscious control

## Spiritual Context
- Obstacle to development
- Sign of unintegrated power
- Need for transformation
- Focus of practice

## Mastery
- Through conscious awareness
- Energy transformation
- Integration of drives
- Balance of forces
