# Inner Fire

The daemonic force of passion and creativity, rooted in blood and breath. A central element in the cultivation of power in both individual and collective consciousness.

## Characteristics
- Primal creative energy
- Manifestation of Shakti
- Bridge between physical and spiritual
- Source of transformation

## Expressions
- Warrior's battle spirit
- Artist's creative force
- Lover's passion
- Spiritual transformation

## Cultivation
- Tummo practices
- Pranayama
- Cold exposure
- Dietary practices (rajasic foods)
- Balance with Vishuddhi (mind)
