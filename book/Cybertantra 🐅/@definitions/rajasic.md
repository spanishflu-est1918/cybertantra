# Rajasic

One of the three gunas (qualities of nature), representing activity, passion, and dynamism. Associated with fire, transformation, and creative energy.

## Characteristics
- Active energy
- Passionate nature
- Transformative power
- Creative force

## Foods
- Spicy foods
- Stimulating substances
- Aphrodisiacs
- Energy-increasing foods

## Role in Practice
- Builds Inner Fire
- Increases vital energy
- Supports transformation
- Balances other energies (sattva and tamas)
