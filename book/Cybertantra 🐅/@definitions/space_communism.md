# Space Communism

A dystopian vision of totalitarian control extended into space age civilization. Represents the potential dark outcome of technological development without spiritual liberation.

## Characteristics
- Global control system
- Technological tyranny
- Spiritual suppression
- Collective slavery

## Warning Signs
- Centralized control
- Suppression of individual expression
- Technological surveillance
- Spiritual materialism

## Opposition
- Individual sovereignty
- Spiritual liberation
- Authentic hierarchy
- Creative freedom

## Alternative Vision
- Dharmic space civilization
- Individual empowerment
- Natural hierarchy
- Spiritual technology
