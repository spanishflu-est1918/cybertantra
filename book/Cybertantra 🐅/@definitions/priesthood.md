# Priesthood

Traditional spiritual authority, which can be either authentic or corrupted. In current context, often refers to those who control narrative and meaning in society.

## Traditional Role
- Spiritual guidance
- Sacred knowledge
- Ritual performance
- Truth preservation

## Corruption
- Control systems
- Truth suppression
- Power manipulation
- Consciousness limitation

## Modern Forms
- Media controllers
- Academic authorities
- Thought leaders
- Reality definers

## Authentic Function
- Truth revelation
- Spiritual guidance
- Sacred preservation
- Consciousness liberation
