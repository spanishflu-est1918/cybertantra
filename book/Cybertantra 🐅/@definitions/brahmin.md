# Brahmin/Brahmanical

Originally referring to those naturally inclined to philosophical and spiritual pursuits. In its pure form, represents the highest expression of intellectual and spiritual development.

## Traditional Role
- Teachers and philosophers
- Guardians of knowledge
- Spiritual guides
- Intellectual leaders

## Corruption
- Hereditary privilege
- Empty intellectualism
- Spiritual materialism
- Loss of authentic power

## Modern Context
- Often degraded to mere intellectualism
- Need for authentic wisdom
- Balance of knowledge and experience
- Return to true spiritual authority
