# Bhakti

The path of devotional spirituality, characterized by deep love and surrender to the divine. A fundamental approach to spiritual development through emotional connection.

## Characteristics
- Devotional practice
- Emotional connection to divine
- Path of surrender and love
- Direct heart-centered approach

## Forms
- Personal deity worship
- Devotional singing/chanting
- Sacred ritual and ceremony
- Emotional purification

## Modern Context
- Can transcend intellectual barriers
- Important in Kali Yuga
- Often combined with other practices
- Tool for spiritual transformation
