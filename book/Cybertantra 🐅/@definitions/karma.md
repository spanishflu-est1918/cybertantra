# Karma

The universal law of cause and effect operating on all levels of existence. More than just "good" or "bad" fortune, karma represents the totality of one's actions and their consequences.

## Levels
- Physical actions and consequences
- Mental/emotional patterns and effects
- Spiritual development and results
- Collective/societal patterns

## Types
- Sanchita (accumulated from past lives)
- Prarabdha (currently manifesting)
- Kriyamana (being created now)

## Application
- Guide for ethical action
- Tool for understanding consequences
- Framework for personal development
- Basis for understanding destiny
