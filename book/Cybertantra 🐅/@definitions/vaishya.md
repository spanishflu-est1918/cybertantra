# Vaishya

The merchant-creator caste in traditional society, representing those naturally oriented toward wealth creation, trade, and material organization.

## Characteristics
- Creative enterprise
- Resource management
- Value creation
- Practical innovation

## Traditional Role
- Commerce
- Agriculture
- Wealth creation
- Resource distribution

## Modern Context
- Entrepreneurship
- Economic creation
- Innovation
- Resource development

## Virtues
- Initiative
- Productivity
- Fair dealing
- Material wisdom
- Creative enterprise
