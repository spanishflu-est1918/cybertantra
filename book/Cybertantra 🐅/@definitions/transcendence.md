# Transcendence

The act or state of rising above material reality to access higher spiritual realms. In spiritual practice, transcendence represents movement beyond ordinary consciousness toward divine awareness.

## Characteristics
- Movement beyond physical limitations
- Rising above material consciousness
- Access to higher spiritual states
- Often opposed to immanence

## Practice
- Achieved through devotion (bhakti)
- Requires spiritual discipline
- Can be balanced with immanence
- Important in Kali Yuga for maintaining spiritual connection

## Modern Context
- Challenging in materialistic age
- Often misunderstood or oversimplified
- Key to spiritual development
- Must be balanced with practical reality
