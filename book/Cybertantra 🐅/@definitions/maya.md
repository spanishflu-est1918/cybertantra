# Maya

The principle of illusion or measured reality that both veils and reveals ultimate truth. Not merely deception, but the creative power that manifests the world.

## Aspects
- Creative illusion
- Measured reality
- Divine power
- World-manifesting force

## Function
- Creates apparent reality
- Veils ultimate truth
- Enables experience
- Provides field for growth

## Practice
- Understanding its nature
- Working with its power
- Seeing through illusion
- Using it consciously
