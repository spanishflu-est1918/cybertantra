# Kali

The fierce manifestation of Shakti, representing the ultimate power of destruction and creation. Despite her terrifying imagery (garland of heads, blood-smeared form), Kali embodies both destructive and maternal aspects.

## Key Aspects
- Represents necessary destruction for creation
- Operates on principle of energy transformation
- Most active during Kali Yuga
- Associated with beauty through contrast
- Breaks down stagnant forces for renewal

## Historical Context
- Predates Vedic texts
- Connected to primordial mother energy
- Central to understanding current age (Kali Yuga)
