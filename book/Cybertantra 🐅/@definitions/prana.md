# Prana

The vital life force or energy that permeates all existence. In yogic and tantric traditions, prana is the foundation of all life and consciousness.

## Forms
- Physical vitality
- Mental energy
- Spiritual force
- Creative power

## Cultivation
- Breathing practices (pranayama)
- Yoga asanas
- Meditation
- Dietary practices

## Significance
- Essential life force
- Basis of spiritual practice
- Connection between body and mind
- Tool for transformation
