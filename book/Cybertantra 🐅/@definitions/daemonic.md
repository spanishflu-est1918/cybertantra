# Daemonic

A primal creative force or spirit that drives transformation and development. Not to be confused with "demonic" in the negative sense.

## Characteristics
- Creative force
- Transformative power
- Divine inspiration
- Natural genius

## Manifestations
- Artistic inspiration
- Creative passion
- Spiritual power
- Transformative energy

## Role in Practice
- Source of power
- Creative force
- Transformative agent
- Connection to divine
