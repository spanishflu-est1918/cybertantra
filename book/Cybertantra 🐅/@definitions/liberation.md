# Liberation

The state of spiritual freedom and self-realization. In tantric practice, involves freedom from both material and spiritual limitations while maintaining conscious power.

## Aspects
- Freedom from limitation
- Conscious power
- Self-realization
- Integration of opposites

## Paths
- Through direct experience
- Through energy transformation
- Through conscious action
- Through integration

## Modern Context
- Freedom from conditioning
- Authentic power
- Personal sovereignty
- Spiritual autonomy
