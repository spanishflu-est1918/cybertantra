# Manipura Chakra

The third primary energy center (chakra) located at the solar plexus, associated with personal power, transformation, and the element of fire.

## Characteristics
- Seat of expression and personal power
- Connected to Inner Fire (agni)
- Center of transformation
- Associated with will and determination

## Practice
- Activated through specific breathing practices (pranayama)
- Balanced through integration with Vishuddhi
- Strengthened by rajasic foods
- Key to managing creative and transformative energy
