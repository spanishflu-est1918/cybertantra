### Master Ages and Yuga Cycles

Within Tantra, we recognize that time unfolds through two overarching eras - The Master Age of Shakti and the Master Age of Shiva. The master Age of Shiva is associated with the Satya Yuga and the Treta Yuga. The Yugas are these massive expanses of time. And these yugas express a certain type of consciousness.

Within the Satya Yuga, we have an age of pure spirit. An age of oneness, where is everything collected in the monistic point. Some people might call this the Godhead. And in the Treta Yuga, we have everything represented in mind. This is God reflecting upon himself.

We currently exist in the master Age of Shakti, that is the age of materiality, which includes the Dvapara Yuga, the age of fire and creation, and the current Yuga, the Kali Yuga. That is the age of blood and materiality. And so everything that we experience within the Kali Yuga has its basis in the blood, in the material.

As we progress in this active age, it is important to understand that all consciousness begins in the material and is built upon that foundation.
